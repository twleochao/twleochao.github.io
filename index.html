Research Focus

Modern AI excels at generation but often fails at process control. My work aims to bridge this gap by designing Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) systems that are observable, steerable, and reliable. Whether I am measuring cognitive offloading in education (ARCALA) or reducing latency in real-time decision tools (HeadsUp), my goal is to ensure AI remains a tool for human agency, not a replacement for it.
